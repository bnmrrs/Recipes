# Mom's BBQ sauce

## Ingredients
* 1 cup ketchup
* 1/3 cup soy sauce
* 1/3 cup liquid honey
* 3-4 minced garlic cloves
* 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
* 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
* 1 tbsp chili powder
* 1 tsp cumin
* 1 tsp celery seed (not salt)
* 1/4 cup lemon juice

# Directions
* Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
