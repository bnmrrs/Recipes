# Guacamole

## Ingredients
* 1 Avocado
* 1 lime
* Pinch of salt
* One roma tomato
* Pinch of chili powder

# Directions
* In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, chili powder, and salt
* Mix in the chopped tomato
* Refrigerate for 1 hour
